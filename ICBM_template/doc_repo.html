Document Repository


As a disclaimer, most of the documents you see here are not in their final states. The development team endeavors to maintain professional standard writing, grammar, and content, but without extensive proofreading small errors do slip through the cracks.

Project Documents:
  • Instructions for First Time Use: Simple sequence of steps to allow users to add hours to their timesheet.
  • Software Design Document (SDD): A complete description of TARS, its purpose, and its components.
  • Complete Requirements Document: Excel Spreadsheet of Customer Requirements
  • Requirements Summary: Summarized Customer Requirements, including current statuses as of 5/8/2012.
